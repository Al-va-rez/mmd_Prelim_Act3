    Discover the Magic of Siquijor

    Siquijor, known as the "Mystical Island," is a paradise of white-sand beaches, turquoise waters, and lush waterfalls. Beyond its natural beauty, it carries a unique charm with its folk healing traditions and enchanting history. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Siquijor has something for everyone.

    • Relax on pristine beaches and clear waters
    • Visit Cambugahay Falls and the Balete Tree
    • Experience local folklore and healing rituals

    Discover the wonders of Siquijor and immerse yourself in an island filled with mystery, beauty, and adventure. Let the magic of this hidden gem leave you with unforgettable memories.

    Check out some of the most popular and highly-sought after attractions in Siquijor

    Cambugahay Falls – Nature’s Three-Tiered Wonder

    Take a refreshing dip in crystal-clear waters or swing into adventure at this stunning waterfall.

    Salagdoong Beach – Cliff Diving Paradise

    Feel the adrenaline rush as you leap off towering cliffs into the turquoise sea.

    Old Enchanted Balete Tree – The Mystical Giant

    Visit this centuries-old tree and experience its natural fish spa at the spring below.

    Lazi Church & Convent – A Glimpse of History

    Step back in time with this Spanish-era church and one of the oldest convents in the country.

    Paliton Beach – Siquijor’s Secret White Sand Escape

    Relax on powdery white sands and watch breathtaking sunsets over the horizon.

    Cantabon Cave – A Subterranean Adventure

    Crawl, wade, and explore the depths of Siquijor’s most fascinating cave system.

    Guiwanon Spring Park – Serenity in Nature

    Walk along wooden pathways over mangroves and enjoy the peaceful ocean breeze.

    Kagusuan Beach – Hidden Gem of Siquijor

    Discover a secluded paradise with dramatic rock formations and untouched beauty.

    Mt. Bandilaan National Park – Summit of Tranquility

    Hike to the island’s highest point and soak in panoramic views of Siquijor.
